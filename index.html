Frequently Asked Questions

What are peptides? +
Peptides are molecules made by linking two or more amino acids together. When a chain contains fewer than around 50 amino acids, it is a peptide. They perform a wide range of biological functions and are used in scientific research and wellness applications.
How should I store my peptides? +
Store peptides in a refrigerator at 2–8°C to preserve stability and effectiveness. Once reconstituted, use within 30 days and keep refrigerated. Pre-filled pens should be brought to room temperature before use.

Use our online dosage calculator which accounts for your body weight, pen concentration, and desired dose to give you a precise measurement every time.
